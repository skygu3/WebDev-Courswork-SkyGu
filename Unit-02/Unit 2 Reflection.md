# Unit 2 Reflection

[Reflect on your journey here]
In this unit, I learned about design thinking and how it applies to web design. Designing is about solving a problem for the users. The process of empathizing and thinking about user needs helped me see design in a more structured way.
One of the most useful parts was learning about UI and UX. UI is about how the website looks, while UX is about how it works and how easy it is to use. I realized that even if a website looks good, it can still be frustrating if the user experience is bad. This made me pay more attention to how I interact with websites in daily life.
I chose Kai as my client because his focus on art and nature gave me a clear direction for design ideas, which resonates with me. Working with a client profile also helped me understand the importance of designing for a specific person instead of just making something I like. 
Overall, this unit helped me understand that good web design is a mix of creativity and problem-solving, not just making things looking good.
