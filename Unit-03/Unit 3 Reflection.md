# Unit 3 Reflection

[Reflect on your journey here]
In this unit, I focused more on the planning and designing process of a website. The brainstorming helped me turn my client’s ideas into something more concrete. Instead of just thinking generally, I started to consider specific things like layout, colors, how the content would be organized and the overall appearance it will express to the users.
Imagining how the website would look before building it is very essential. Having a clear idea in mind would make it easier to stay consistent with the design. So keeping the client in mind is really important in order not to mess up the design.Another point I learned is that simple designs are often better. Adding too many elements can make the website feel too crowded.
Overall, this unit helped me understand how to turn ideas into a real plan. It made the whole web design process feel more organized instead of just randomly building a page. We need to cherry-pick.
